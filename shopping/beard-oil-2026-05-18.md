## tl;dr

**Budget:** Bulldog (£3.65 Tesco Clubcard)
**Best conditioning:** Honest Amish (~£12.99)
**UK indie:** Shakesbeard or Percy Nobleman
**Lightweight/premium:** Jack Black (~£22.50)
**DIY:** Plain jojoba oil (~£5–8/100ml)

Multiple forum users prefer **balm over oil** for beards over ~2cm — less greasy, better hold.

## Oil vs Balm

Multiple forum users across Singletrack World and Rokslide prefer **beard balm** over oil:
- Balm gives hold + conditioning; oil gives conditioning only
- Oil can feel greasy on longer beards
- **Recommendation:** Oil for short beards (under ~2cm), balm or both for longer

Balm alternatives mentioned: Bulldog Beard Balm, Grizzly Balm, Honest Amish Beard Balm, Beardbrand Utility Balm
