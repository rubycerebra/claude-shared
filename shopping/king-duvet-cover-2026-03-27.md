## Reddit Consensus Notes

- **Sateen vs Percale:** Sateen = silky, warm, drapes well. Percale = crisp, cool, hotel-like. Hot sleepers → percale or linen.
- **Thread count:** 300-400TC is the sweet spot. Above 600TC often marketing fluff.
- **Linen trend growing:** More users switching to linen for temperature regulation, especially hot sleepers.
- **Dunelm own-brand declining:** Multiple reports of quality drop. Dorma line (sold at Dunelm) remains reliable.
- **M&S sales strategy:** Never pay full price — bedding sales happen every 6-8 weeks.
- **IKEA sizing note:** 240x220cm is EU king — check your duvet insert dimensions.
- **Christy** and **Bedeck** also well-regarded premium options but less frequently mentioned.
- **La Redoute** recommended as affordable linen alternative to Piglet in Bed.
- **TK Maxx** — worth checking for discounted branded covers (~£50 for premium brands).
