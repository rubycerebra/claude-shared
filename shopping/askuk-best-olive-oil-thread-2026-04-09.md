## Thread-Wide Warnings

1. **Adulteration risk** — "A third of all olive oils sold in the UK in 2020 were actually lower quality oil" (commenter cited report). Widespread fraud particularly in Spanish and Italian supply chains.
2. **Taste is subjective** — Thread consensus: buy a small bottle first before committing to bulk.
3. **Grassy ≠ bad** — some commenters noted confusion between quality flavour notes and "off" taste; peppery/grassy finish = high polyphenols = fresh/quality.
4. **Sedimentation = quality signal** — small amount of sediment at bottle bottom is a positive indicator in unfiltered oils.

---

## Quality Signals Valued by This Thread

- Extra virgin, cold-pressed
- Single origin (country or estate)
- Greek origin frequently mentioned as "milder/smoother" vs Italian (more peppery)
- First pressing
- Dark glass bottles
- Visible sedimentation in unfiltered variants
