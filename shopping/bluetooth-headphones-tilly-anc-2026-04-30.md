### ❌ Skip These

- **Generic no-brand kids headphones under £15** — flimsy hinges, cables fail fast, no proper volume limiting
- **Adult headphones at this price point** — no volume limiting, not sized for small heads, will hurt after an hour
- **Anything marketed as "cat ear" novelty headphones** — battery and build are almost always poor

### Key Takeaways

- **Best pick overall:** EarFun K4NC (~£27) — only kids headphone under £30 with genuine ANC; battery and hearing protection are exceptional
- **If you need it today:** JLab JBuddies Studio 2 from Currys (~£25) — Pink or Blue, in-store pickup
- **If Tilly chooses:** PowerLocus Bobo (~£20) — LED lights and bright colours will appeal at that age
- **Hearing protection note:** At 6, the 85dB volume limit on all three picks is important — prolonged use above that causes permanent hearing damage. The EarFun K4NC's 74dB setting is the safest for long car journeys.
