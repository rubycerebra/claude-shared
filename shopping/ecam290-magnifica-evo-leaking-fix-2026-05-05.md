**Original post (German, translated):** "Can someone tell me what the spare part is called and where I could get it? Something is leaking and running past the red drip tray. Or does anyone have experience with how to fix this?"

---

## Summary

A DeLonghi ECAM290.81 Magnifica Evo owner reported leaking past the red drip tray area. The thread confirmed the culprit is the **expansion chamber** (Aufdampfkammer) and its seals/O-rings. The fix is: disassemble the expansion chamber connection, clean off scale/residue, apply silicone grease — and replace the thin seal if worn. The OP confirmed this worked.

---

## Key Themes

- **Expansion chamber** (black plastic piece above the red drip area) is the likely leak source
- Scale and residue build-up around the expansion chamber connection causes the seal to fail
- Silicone grease applied to the connection/seal area stops the leak temporarily
- The thin seal/gasket itself can be found and ordered — replacement gives the best long-term result
- Disassembly is "a bit complex" but manageable without a service centre

---

## Consensus

Clean the expansion chamber connection first (descale, remove residue), then apply **food-safe silicone grease** to the seal/gasket area. This resolved the leak for the OP. The thin seal itself should be replaced when convenient — it can be sourced from spare parts shops.
