## ❌ Commonly Warned Against
- **Supermarket own-brands** — thin material, poor longevity
- **TK Maxx own-brands** — quality wildly inconsistent
- **Tokyo Laundry / fast fashion boxers** — wear out fast

---

## Cotton Quality Ladder

| Grade | Example | Feel vs Regular Cotton |
|---|---|---|
| Standard cotton | Primark, supermarkets | Baseline |
| Pure cotton (woven) | M&S, Calvin Klein | Notably softer, breathes well |
| Long-staple cotton | Sunspel Poplin | Silkier, stronger, less pilling |
| Supima cotton | Uniqlo | Top 1% US cotton — noticeably finer |
| Cotton batiste | Derek Rose | Lightweight weave — cool and luxurious |
| Sea Island cotton | Sunspel premium range | Rarest, softest cotton available |

---

## 💡 Key Takeaways
- **Best everyday:** M&S 5-pack at £28 — pure cotton, £5.60/pair, zero drama
- **Step up:** CK 3-packs from Amazon or M&M Direct — ~£8/pair, 7+ year track record
- **Premium:** Sunspel at £47 — long-staple poplin cotton, British heritage, buy on multi-buy
- **Budget-premium:** Uniqlo Supima ~£13 — genuinely superior cotton without the premium price
