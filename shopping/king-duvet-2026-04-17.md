## Top UK-Relevant Picks

### 1. The Wool Room 🇬🇧
- Mentioned in the UK duvet thread as high quality, warm, lightweight, no spiky feathers
- Good for sensory-sensitive sleepers
- Flat/blanket feel rather than cloud-puff — warm but not fluffy
- **Buy:** woolroom.com

### 2. Devon Duvets 🇬🇧
- UK brand, wool fill, 15+ tog very warm
- "Super warm but heavy" — reviewer noted a single might be easier to get into a cover
- Not cloud-like; wool is flat. Good for cold rooms
- **Buy:** devonduvets.co.uk

### 3. IKEA FJÄLLBRÄCKA
- Multiple mentions across threads — budget champion
- Extra warm version recommended for cold sleepers
- Washes well (important for king size — check your machine capacity)
- Get one size up from your bed for the fluffy draped look
- **Buy:** ikea.com/gb — approx £60–80 king

### 4. Sweet Dreams Direct (eBay/Amazon) 🇬🇧
- Loose-fill wool-style construction — "almost consumed me, both thick and heavy"
- Sells on eBay and Amazon UK
- Good for those who want weight without weighted blanket

### 5. Fine Bedding Co / Night Lark (coverless) 🇬🇧
- UK brand, coverless duvets — no need to wrestle a cover on
- Pricey but mentioned positively for ease of use
- **Buy:** finebedding.co.uk

## The Fluffy Hack (most upvoted tip — 135 pts)
> "The trick to making it fluffy is to buy a **king size duvet** with a **slightly smaller cover**. It fills it up more."

Widely confirmed. Also: two duvets stacked inside one cover for maximum fluff (22 pts).

## Fill Type Consensus

| Fill | Pros | Cons | UK Availability |
|------|------|------|-----------------|
| Duck down | Fluffy, light, warm | Spiky quills, allergies | ✓ John Lewis, M&S |
| Goose down | Fluffier, denser clusters | Expensive | ✓ JL, Devon Duvets |
| Wool | Breathable, temp-regulating, heavy | Flat, not cloud-like | ✓ Wool Room, Devon Duvets |
| Synthetic | Washable, cheap, hypoallergenic | Gets warm, less fluffy | ✓ Everywhere |

- **Cold sleeper / cold room**: Wool (Devon Duvets, Wool Room) or high-tog down
- **Fluffy cloud look**: Down or duck down, size up, smaller cover
- **Hypoallergenic**: Synthetic (IKEA) or wool
- **Best value**: IKEA FJÄLLBRÄCKA

## Tog Guide
- **4.5 tog** — summer
- **10.5 tog** — all-seasons standard
- **13.5–15 tog** — winter / cold rooms / cold sleepers
